Pace Your Pulse

Description

Pace Your Pulse is a fitness application which allows its users to work out at a desired heart rate, without the need for manual heart rate monitoring and active pace adaptation. By precisely timing music beats with respect to the user's pace, that pace can be influenced subconsciously to increase or decrease. With this technique, Pace Your Pulse aims to guide the user's workout intensity and bodily strain, and consequently guide their heart rate towards a desired range.

“Let the beat guide your pace and heart rate.”
